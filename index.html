Introduction¶

In this project, I aim to investigate cancer incidence rates in the United States. My research inquiry centers on discerning cancer incidence variations concerning gender, age, and race across years.

I employ data from the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) spanning 1999 to 2020. This dataset contains comprehensive information on cancer types and patients' demographic characteristics, such as age, gender, region, and cancer incidence rates.

It's possible that some individuals who had cancer also contracted COVID-19, and when they passed away, their deaths may have been attributed to COVID-19.
